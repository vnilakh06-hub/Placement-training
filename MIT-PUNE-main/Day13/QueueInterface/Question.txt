Scenario

An airport receives landing requests from multiple aircraft.

Every aircraft has a priority level:

Priority 1 → Medical Emergency
Priority 2 → Fuel Emergency
Priority 3 → VIP Flight
Priority 4 → Commercial Flight

The runway should always be allocated to the aircraft having the highest priority (lowest priority number).
